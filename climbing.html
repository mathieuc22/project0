Climbing

Even if I would love to do some traditional climbing and spend lots of time outdoor... practicing rock climbing in a big city is quite difficult and indoor climbing is almost the only alternative.

But don't get me wrong, sport climbing is a lot of fun even indoor. It's very challenging, you can work on strengh and flexibility, and you can go whenever you want no matter the weather.

My climbing gear
