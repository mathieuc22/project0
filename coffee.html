Coffee

Few years ago I discovered slow coffee and bought me a Hario V60 Brewer. The result is very good... but maybe not the first time, the pour over method is not easy.

Now it's part of my daily routine and I'd like to share with you my recipe.

What do you need?

Yes you need some tools, but not that much:

  • a Hario V60 dripper
  • a paper coffee filter
  • a water kettle
  • fresh grounds of coffee
  • a grinder

Pour method

Now take some time and follow those steps:

  1. Rinse
  2. Grind
  3. Bloom
  4. Pour
  5. Enjoy your cup of coffee...
